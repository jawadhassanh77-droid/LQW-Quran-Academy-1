Why Tajweed Matters Beyond Just Reading Quran

Tajweed is the science of correct Quran pronunciation. Correct Tajweed ensures that Arabic letters are articulated properly from their origin points. Incorrect pronunciation can sometimes change the meaning of verses, which is why professional guidance is essential.
