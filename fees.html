Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling

Yes! We offer a 3-day free trial with no payment required. You can experience our teaching methodology, meet your assigned teacher, and evaluate if our classes are the right fit for you or your child — completely risk-free.

Absolutely. We have a dedicated team of certified, qualified, and experienced female Quran teachers available for sisters and young girls. All female teachers hold Ijazah certifications and are fluent in English. You can request a female teacher during enrollment.

Each class is 30 minutes long by default. If you prefer longer sessions, we can accommodate 45-minute or 60-minute classes upon request — please discuss this with our team when enrolling to adjust pricing accordingly.

Yes, all plans include free rescheduling. If you miss a class or need to reschedule, simply inform your teacher or our admin team via WhatsApp at least 2 hours before the scheduled time. We will arrange a make-up class at a convenient time for you.

Yes! We offer a 10% sibling discount when you enroll two or more children from the same family. This discount applies from the second child onward. Contact us on WhatsApp to set up a family package and save on your monthly fees.

No special software is required. Our classes are conducted via Zoom, Skype, or Google Meet — whichever is most convenient for you. All you need is a stable internet connection, a device with a camera and microphone (tablet, laptop, or smartphone), and the free video call app of your choice.

We welcome students of all ages — from young children as young as 4 years old to adults and elderly learners. We also have specialized programs for reverts to Islam who are starting from scratch. Our teachers are trained to adapt their teaching style for every age group.

Monthly fees are paid securely via bank transfer, PayPal, or Wise (TransferWise). Payment is due at the start of each month. We accept payments in GBP £, USD $, CAD $, AUD $, and EUR €. Our team will send you payment details after your free trial.

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or change your plan at any time. Simply contact our team on WhatsApp and we will adjust your plan starting from the next billing cycle. There are no penalties or fees for changing your plan — total flexibility is part of our commitment to you.

Yes! Students on the Standard, Premium, and Weekend Elite plans receive a digital completion certificate upon finishing their course or milestone (e.g., completing Noorani Qaida, finishing the Quran, or completing Hifz). Certificates are issued with the teacher's signature and academy stamp.
